    About EQhigh

    Emotional Intelligence, or Emotional Quotient, is the ability to identify and manage emotions effectively. It is one of the most crucial skills in the 21st Century. Yet, many institutions don’t educate their communities on this skill, which holds substantial importance than IQ in causing personal growth. EQHigh aims to raise awareness on EQ in today’s generation by aiding the development of this skill to help them achieve success.
